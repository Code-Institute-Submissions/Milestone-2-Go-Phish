How To Play

Welcome to Go Phish, a memory matching game aimed at educating people of the threat of cybercrime!

The game has 4 difficulty levels to choose from: Easy, Medium, Hard and Impossible.

The Aim of the game is to match each card with their matching card to create pairs before the timer runs out. Each card pair represent a type of phishing. Click here to learn more about the different types. Simply click on a card to flip it. Remember, you can only flip 2 cards at a time so remember where the cards you have flipped are located in the deck.

Best of luck!
